Algorithms Used in the Project

Graph Representation

We represent the city as a weighted graph. Roads are edges, landmarks are nodes.

Dijkstra's Algorithm

Used for shortest path calculations between zones.

BFS / DFS

Used for traversal and connectivity analysis.

MST Algorithms

Prim’s or Kruskal’s algorithm used for optimal infrastructure planning.
